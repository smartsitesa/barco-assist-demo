About Barco Assist

A dedicated service partner for sites that need consistent maintenance, cleaner environments and stronger operational control.

Barco Assist focuses on the realities of ongoing property care: recurring maintenance needs, hygiene standards, site performance and visibility for owners or managers who need things handled properly. The work is centred on environments where neglected issues quickly become costly, disruptive or visible to tenants, customers and stakeholders.

That makes Barco Assist valuable for facilities managers, landlords, body corporates, retail operators and property owners who want reliable support across the lifecycle of the site.
